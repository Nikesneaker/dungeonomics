Streamlined Roleplaying

Dungeonomics is a place where DMs can manage their campaigns without the hassle of sifting through a mountain of paper. DMs can manage all aspects of their campaign from plot creation and setting up encounters to looking up/creating monsters or NPCs.

Just so we're clear, we believe that there's no substitute for the real thing. Getting together with other adventurers in person will always be the best way to play D&D. Our goal is to enhance that and make it easier for you to immerse yourself in the game by eliminating the need to flip through a bunch of papers, which can sometimes pull you out of the experience.

Now, go forth and create! Also, please come check us out on Reddit to give us feedback and to receive updates.

Create a Campaign

Here, you can enter your notes and create chapters as your party progresses through the plot, allowing for a seamless progression.

Tag Monsters and NPCs

Manually adding monsters and NPCs to your campaign is a thing of the past. With Dungeonomics, you can tag Monstesr and NPCs in your notes to quickly and easily pull up their stats.

Link to Characters

When you've tagged a Monster or NPC in your campaign, it will automatically create a link to that character in your campaign. All you have to do is click that generated link and it will take you to your Monster or NPC statblock.

Create Monsters and NPCs

Using our Monster and NPC tools, you can create your own characters to use in your campaigns. We have created this in such a way that it allows you to input all the information you'll need for any Monster or NPC in any RPG system, not just D&D.

Wiki and Community

We have a wiki that is constantly being updated to help with any questions you have. We also have thousands of registered users, and the generous support of the reddit community.
